02 / Auto

Stop Paying Dealership Prices.

Losing your car keys used to mean a trip to the local hardware store and a quick grind on a brass blank. Today, modern vehicle security has turned car keys into sophisticated microcomputers. And the dealerships are capitalizing on this heavily.

The Dealership Monopoly Myth

When you lose your transponder key or smart fob, the dealership will happily tell you that only they can replace it. They will require you to tow your car to their lot, wait in the service lounge for hours, and then hand you an absurdly inflated bill. You aren't just paying for the key; you're paying for their showroom overhead.

"You are paying for the badge on the building, not the technology in the key."

The Mobile Advantage

The truth is, an experienced automotive locksmith uses the exact same OEM diagnostic computers and programming software that the dealerships use. The difference? We bring the shop to you.

If you're stranded in a parking lot in Southfield or your driveway in Detroit, EZ Locksmith arrives on-site. We cut the physical blade with precision CNC machines right in our van, connect to your car's OBD-II port, and program the transponder chip directly to your vehicle's immobilizer system. It's done in a fraction of the time, for a fraction of the cost.

Don't Call a Tow Truck. Call Us.

Whether you need a spare proximity fob for a Push-to-Start ignition or you've completely lost all your keys, bypass the dealership waiting room entirely. Save your money, save your time, and get back on the road instantly.
